🤖 AI & Machine Learning — Career Program

A job-focused, mentor-led program designed to take you from learning to earning with hands-on projects, guided mentorship, and placement backing.

What the program includes

  • 📘 Structured Program A carefully designed curriculum covering AI & Machine Learning fundamentals to practical, industry-relevant skills—built to make you job-ready, not just certificate-ready.
  • 👨‍🏫 12 Expert Mentor Sessions (1 per week) Weekly live sessions with 2 industry experts (Tech & Professional) to explain concepts, solve doubts, review progress, and share real-world use cases. This ensures consistent learning and accountability.
  • 🧭 Personal Learning Manager You get a dedicated learning manager to track your progress, guide your journey, and keep you aligned with your career goals—so you never feel lost or stuck.
  • 🌐 Private Learning Community (Exclusive Access) Join a focused peer & mentor community where 5–15 opportunities are shared daily—internships, projects, jobs, collaborations, and learning resources.
  • 🎯 2 Weeks Placement Training Intensive preparation for interviews: resume building, mock interviews, technical + HR rounds, AI interview practice, and confidence building.
  • 🚀 2 Weeks Placement Drive Direct access to hiring opportunities with structured placement support to convert your skills into a job offer.
